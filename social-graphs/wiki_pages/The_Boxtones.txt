'''The Boxtones''' is a Dubai-based [[Rock music|rock]] band composed of Scottish and Canadian musicians.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Filoromo|first1=Maura|title=The Up-and-Drumming Dubai Scene: Q&A with The Boxtones’ Gill Tierney|url=http://tomtommag.com/2014/09/boxtones/|publisher=[[Tom Tom Magazine]]|accessdate=26 February 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Garratt|first1=Rob|title=Boxtones in Dubai (review)|url=http://www.timeoutdubai.com/nightlife/features/42029-boxtones-in-dubai|publisher=Time Out Dubai|accessdate=26 February 2015}}</ref>

Throughout 2014 and 2015, the band played at a few high-profile concerts that saw them perform alongside numerous international superstars such as [[Pharrell Williams]], [[The Who]], [[Stereophonics]], [[Lily Allen]], [[Jason Derulo]], [[The Parlotones]], [[Richard Ashcroft]], [[Razorlight]], [[The Ting Tings]] and 80's music icons [[Europe (band)|Europe]].<ref name="Rock legends Europe to perform live in Dubai" /><ref name="Boxtones GBW National" /><ref name="Dubai band The Boxtones sign to record label Universal!">{{cite web|last1=Steadman |first1=Lauren |title= Dubai band The Boxtones sign to record label Universal! |url=http://www.ahlanlive.com/dubai-band-the-boxtones-sign-to-record-label-universal-558408.html |publisher=Ahlan! |accessdate=9 January 2016}}</ref>

On 25 February 2015, The Boxtones signed a record deal with [[Universal Music Group]] Middle East & North Africa (MENA).<ref name="Dubai band The Boxtones sign to record label Universal!" /><ref>{{cite web |last1=Davey |first1=Caitlyn |title=Growing stronger: Dubai band The Boxtones sign deal with Universal Studios|url=http://7daysindubai.com/growing-stronger-dubai-band-boxtones-sign-deal-universal-studios/57979 |publisher=7days in Dubai |access-date=18 March 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150523141301/http://7daysindubai.com/growing-stronger-dubai-band-boxtones-sign-deal-universal-studios |archive-date=2015-05-23 |url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Hamad |first1=Marwa |title=Soundbites: The Boxtones Signed By Universal |url=https://www.zawya.com/story/Soundbites_The_Boxtones_signed_by_Universal-GN_16032015_170304/ |publisher=Thomson Reuters Zawya |access-date=17 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| last1=Garratt |first1=Rob |title=Music going underground in the UAE |url=https://www.pressreader.com/uae/the-national-news-the-review/20170121/282067686641637 |publisher=The National |access-date=9 August 2017}}</ref>

The Boxtones’ debut album ''Home'' was released on 25 November 2016 through Universal Music MENA. So far, singles "Against the Odds",<ref name="People Against the Odds">{{cite web|last1=Ayra|first1=Kritika|title=PEOPLE: Against The Odds – The Boxtones have made it|url=http://www.dubaiweek.ae/people/17559/against-the-odds-the-boxtones-have-made-it/|publisher=Dubai Week|access-date=5 September 2016}}</ref><ref name="Dubai rockers The Boxtones go against the odds">{{cite web|last1=Hamad|first1=Marwa|title=Dubai rockers The Boxtones go against the odds|url=http://gulfnews.com/life-style/music/dubai-rockers-the-boxtones-go-against-the-odds-1.1905911|access-date=7 October 2016}}</ref> “City of Mirrors” (featuring [[Dubai]]-based rapper Two-Tone),<ref name="Soundbites: New music from UAE artists to check out">{{cite web|last1=Hamad|first1=Marwa|title=Soundbites: New music from UAE artists to check out|url=http://gulfnews.com/life-style/music/soundbites-new-music-from-uae-artists-to-check-out-1.1925233|access-date=22 November 2016}}</ref> “Home”<ref name="The Boxtones release new album Home">{{cite web|title=The Boxtones release new album Home|url=http://gulfnews.com/life-style/music/the-boxtones-release-new-album-home-1.1935969|publisher=Gulf News|accessdate=21 February 2017}}</ref> and “Do I Look Like Somebody You Could Love”<ref name="Scottish Group to back Elton John in 2017">{{cite web|last1=Stephen|first1=Phyllis|title=Scottish Group to back Elton John in 2017|url=http://www.theedinburghreporter.co.uk/2017/01/scottish-group-to-back-elton-john-in-2017/|publisher=The Edinburgh Reporter|access-date=21 February 2017}}</ref> have been released, each with its own official music video.

In recent years, The Boxtones have performed alongside [[Liam Gallagher]], [[Kaiser Chiefs]], [[Blur (band)|Blur]], [[Florence and the Machine|Florence & The Machine]], [[Enrique Iglesias]], Travis, [[The Chemical Brothers|Chemical Brothers]], [[The Charlatans (English band)|The Charlatans]] and The Temples. They were announced as the support for the [[Elton John]] concert that took place at the 13,500-capacity Dubai Autism Rocks Arena in January 2018, the same venue where they opened for [[Bryan Adams]]
